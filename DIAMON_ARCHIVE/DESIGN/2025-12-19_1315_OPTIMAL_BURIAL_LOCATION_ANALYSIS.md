## I. SITE SELECTION CRITERIA

### Primary Requirements

**1. Geological Stability (2+ million years)**
- **Craton location:** Ancient continental cores (>1 billion years old)
- **Seismic inactivity:** <M4.0 earthquakes in recorded history
- **Non-glaciated:** Avoid ice sheet erosion zones
- **Low erosion rate:** <1mm per 1000 years
- **Above sea level:** +100m minimum (account for future sea level changes)

**2. Monument Triangulation Geometry**
- **Clear lines of sight:** Three non-collinear bearings from monuments
- **Triangulation precision:** <100m error after 2M years of continental drift
- **Monument stability:** All three monuments must remain identifiable

**3. Accessibility (2025-2027 window)**
- **Travel time:** <24 hours from current location
- **Legal access:** Public land or private land with permission
- **Excavation feasibility:** Soil/rock composition allows 5m dig
- **Low surveillance:** Avoid high-security areas

**4. Long-term Markers**
- **Thorium detection:** Soil composition allows gamma penetration
- **Magnetite detection:** Low background magnetic interference
- **Minimal human activity:** Reduce risk of accidental excavation
